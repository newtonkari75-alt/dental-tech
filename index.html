How does AutoBrush® compare to high-end electric toothbrushes for plaque removal?

Unlike traditional electric toothbrushes that clean one tooth at a time and rely on user technique, AutoBrush® cleans all teeth simultaneously. Clinical studies show it removes up to 5.1X more plaque than standard manual brushing by ensuring consistent pressure and optimal coverage across every dental surface in just 30 seconds.

Is AutoBrush® truly ADA-accepted?

Yes. AutoBrush® is currently the only U-shaped toothbrush to receive ADA Acceptance. This distinction is critical because many "knock-off" silicone models on the market have not met the rigorous standards for mechanical cleaning required by the American Dental Association.

Why does AutoBrush® use nylon bristles instead of cheap silicone?

Silicone bristles are too soft to effectively remove hardened plaque and biofilm. AutoBrush® uses patented ultra-soft nylon bristles that mimic the Bass technique recommended by dentists. This provides the necessary mechanical friction to clean deep along the gumline while remaining gentle enough for sensitive tissue.

Can AutoBrush® help prevent gum recession and irritation?

Absolutely. Most gum recession is caused by aggressive manual scrubbing. AutoBrush® uses calibrated sonic vibrations and a sensitive mode paired with red-light therapy to support healthy-looking gums. It eliminates the "human error" of pressing too hard, protecting your enamel and soft tissues.

Is it safe for dental work like braces, crowns, or implants?

Yes, AutoBrush® is engineered to be safe and highly effective for orthodontics and dental work. The U-shaped head fits over braces to clean wires and brackets simultaneously, areas where traditional brushes often leave food particles behind.
